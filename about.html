I started Benevolent Tech because I love working with and helping small businesses, especially those who foster community. Coming from a technical background, I've found my interests and skills best align with helping small businesses with their online communications.

Towards that end, I've dedicated the past 6 years of my professional career to learning as much as I can about online marketing and website technologies which are beautiful inside and out, affordable, easy to use, and boost sales. Think of us as Red Bull for your business.

Here's our 3 step process:

  1. Building you a beautiful, sales-optimized website to guide customers happily down a path to purchasing your product, while at the same time reflecting your brand vision.
  2. Getting your business more attention with custom-tailored search engine optimization, advertising and social media branding.
  3. Continuous improvements based on real-world analytics.

Customers are searching the internet right now for your product and choose competitors because they don't know you exist. Team up now and get found. Not quite ready? Check out our learning center or join our mailing-list.
